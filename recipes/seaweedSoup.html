Seaweed Soup


Description

Seaweed soup(미역국 in Korean) is one of the most popular dishes in Korea.
It is usually served for one's birthday.
Originally, it was served to mothers who just gave birth to their child,
because seaweed and beef is rich of iron and iodide, which are especially essential for pregnant women.
There are variations of seaweed soup, depending on the ingredient that replaces beef, such as clams or sea urchin.

Ingredients

  • Dried seaweed 30g
  • Beef brisket(or anything for stew) 250g
  • Sesame oil(참기름) 1tbsp
  • Water 2L
  • Chopped or minced garlic 1tbsp
  • Soy sauce for soup(국간장) 2tbsp
  • Onion 1
  • Seasoning salt(맛소금) 1/3tbsp

Steps

  1. Cut dried seaweed into pieces and immerse it in water for about 20 minutes.
  2. While the seaweed is being immersed, cut the beef into cubes.
  3. Take out the seaweed pieces and wash them with running water about three times. Then wipe the moisture out.
  4. (No heating yet)Put the sesame oil into the saucepan and mix it with the beef brisket.
  5. Then heat the saucepan to medium until the beef starts to turn brown.
  6. Put the seaweed pieces and stir-fry them for about 30 seconds.
  7. Put the soy sauce and stir-fry them for about 3 minutes.
  8. Pour water into the saucepan and put the onion and the chopped or minced garlic. Boil them for about 30 minuntes with high heat.
  9. Turn down the heat to medium and put the seasing salt. Then heat the soup for additional 5 minutes.
