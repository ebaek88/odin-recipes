Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Kimchi fried rice(김치볶음밥 in Korean) is one of the most popular Korean dishes.
Rice, kimchi and green onion are fried with a choice of incredients, usually Spam or canned tuna.
A fried egg and a sprinkle of seaweed flake are normally added on top of it.

Ingredients

  • Kimchi 250g
  • Steamed rice 200g
  • Green onion 50g
  • Egg 1
  • Spam or canned tuna 100g
  • Cooking oil 2tbsp
  • Red pepper powder 1tbsp
  • Sugar 1tsp
  • Butter 20g
  • Seaweed flake 1tbsp

Steps

  1. Chop green onions. If you are using Spam, cut it into small cubes. Also, chop kimchi into small pieces
  2. Put cooking oil onto the pan and heat it to high.
  3. When the pan is heated enough, put the green onions into the pan and stir-fry them until the scent come out.
  4. Put the Spam or canned tuna into the pan and stir-fry them for about 30 seconds.
  5. Put the chopped kimchi, red pepper powder, and sugar into the pan and stir-fry them for 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. Put the rice into the pan and stir-fry all for 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Put the butter into the pan and stir-fry for 1 to 2 minutes.
  8. Put the fried rice into a bowl, and make a fried egg. Then put the egg and seaweed flake on top of the fried rice.
